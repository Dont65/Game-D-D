## üéÆ Project Overview: AI-Powered D\&D Game

This project is a small **text-based adventure game** inspired by the **Dungeons & Dragons (D\&D)** universe, where **Artificial Intelligence (AI)** acts as the Dungeon Master, generating a dynamic and non-linear storyline.

In this game, you don't just read; you **actively shape the narrative** by making choices that directly impact the course of events, combat, and your character's progression.

### üåü Key Features

  * **Dynamic Storytelling:** The AI creates unique scenarios, characters, and situations, ensuring high replayability.
  * **Immersive D\&D Atmosphere:** Dive into a classic fantasy adventure filled with magic, monsters, and high-stakes decisions.
  * **Easy Setup:** Utilizing **Node.js** and the **Openrouter API**, you can get your own game running locally in just a few steps.
